Privacy Policy

Dishcraft – AI Visual Cooking · Last updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Dishcraft ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile application that transforms food photos and handwritten recipes into AI-generated visual cooking tutorials. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and basic profile information needed to provide our services.

Photos You Upload

When you use Dishcraft, you may upload photos of food dishes or handwritten recipes. These photos are processed by AI services to recognize recipes and generate visual content. Your original photos are stored securely and remain associated with your account.

Generated Content

We store AI-generated images and videos created from your recipes, including step-by-step cooking photos and tutorial videos.

Recipe Data

We store the recipes you create, including dish names, ingredients, cooking steps, and any personal notes you add.

Purchase Information

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We receive purchase confirmation and subscription status through RevenueCat, our subscription management provider.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data such as app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Dishcraft service
  • Process your uploaded photos through AI to recognize recipes
  • Generate AI cooking photos and tutorial videos from your recipes
  • Store your recipes and generated content in your account
  • Manage your subscription and purchase history
  • Improve our app and fix issues
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our features:

  • Supabase – Cloud database and authentication for storing your account, recipes, and generated content
  • Anthropic (Claude) – AI-powered recipe recognition from your uploaded photos
  • Black Forest Labs (Flux) – AI image generation for cooking step photos
  • Runway – AI video generation for cooking tutorial videos
  • RevenueCat – Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Expo / EAS – App build and update delivery

Each third-party service processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Photos sent for AI processing are used solely to generate your requested content and are not used to train AI models.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's cloud infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication, and access controls to protect your information.

Generated images and videos are stored in secure cloud storage and are accessible only to your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete a recipe, the associated data (images, videos) is also deleted. If you delete your account, all your personal data, recipes, and generated content will be permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – View the personal data we hold about you
  • Delete – Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export – Request a copy of your recipe data
  • Correct – Update or correct your personal information
  • Withdraw consent – Stop using the app at any time and request data deletion

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Dishcraft is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Subscriptions and Auto-Renewal

Dishcraft offers auto-renewable subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Subscription payments are processed by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
